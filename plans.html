Common questions

Does this actually work for the STR tax loophole?

Yes — but only if the property qualifies as a short-term rental (avg. guest stay under 7 days) and you materially participate. We help structure the property and operations to meet both requirements from day one.

Do I need to be local to the property?

No. E2E STR was built specifically for out-of-state investors. We coordinate local realtors, vendors, and service providers in the target market on your behalf.

What's included in the guides?

Each guide is a step-by-step action document covering the decisions, vendors, timelines, and checklists for that stage of the launch. They're built from real project experience, not theory.

Can I start with just the guides and add support later?

Absolutely. Download Step 1 for free on the home page, see how we think, and reach out when you're ready to add coordinator support for any stage.
