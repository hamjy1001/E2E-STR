02

Mortgage & Financing

The 15-minute mortgage guide for busy professionals

🏭

DSCR Loans Explained

Most clients land on a DSCR loan — it qualifies on the property's rental income, not your W-2. No tax returns. No debt-to-income headaches. We explain exactly which loan type fits your situation in the first call.

⚖

The Lender Tournament

We request written Loan Estimates from 3–4 lenders simultaneously, compare rate, fees, and penalty terms side by side, then show the top two finalists each other's numbers. "A verbal 'we'll be competitive' means nothing; a real competing offer in their hand means everything."

💰

What Moves Your Rate

Credit score (keep utilization below 10%), down payment tier (25%+ unlocks better rate tiers), and lender competition. On a $500K investment loan, a well-negotiated rate vs. average is often $200–$400/month in cash flow.

📜

Pre-Closing Checklist

No new credit lines. No job changes. No large deposits. We walk you through exactly what not to do in the 60 days before closing — a surprisingly common way investors accidentally kill their own approval.

DIY time: 25–40 hrs → With E2E: ~2–3 hrs (mostly e-signatures)
The mortgage step is where most investors leave money on the table. We run the lender tournament for you. Your job is signing documents; ours is making sure you got the best number.

03

Property Setup

Utilities, security & life-safety compliance — before your first booking

🔢

Smart Security Stack

4 layers: smart locks with unique time-bound codes per reservation, exterior-only cameras, noise/occupancy sensors (privacy-compliant, no audio/video), and leak sensors at kitchen, bathrooms, and laundry. A $150 sensor can prevent a five-figure water-damage claim.

🔥

Life-Safety & Fire Code

STR conversion often triggers hardwired/interconnected smoke detector requirements beyond a standard home sale. We confirm placement in every bedroom, every hallway, and every level — plus CO detectors, extinguisher compliance, and a posted fire escape plan.

⚡

Utilities & Connectivity

Business-grade mesh WiFi on a separate guest network. Accounts transferred into the correct entity name. HVAC serviced before launch. We run a 48-hour dry run with all systems live before the first guest check-in.

✅

9-Point Pre-Go-Live Walkthrough

Smart lock codes tested end-to-end on a guest-facing device. Guest WiFi network isolated. Camera disclosure added to listing. Leak sensors verified. Utilities confirmed in the correct billing name. 48-hour dry run signed off.

DIY time: 14–23 hrs → With E2E: ~2 hrs of approvals
The most common post-launch incidents trace back to setup shortcuts. Lockouts, false alarms, failed inspections, insurance claim denials — all preventable. We check every box before the first guest walks in.

05

Permit Processing

Zoning, licensing & local compliance — navigated for you

📍

Zoning First — Always

A property that can't legally operate as an STR in its zone is a fundamentally different asset than the one you thought you were buying. STR overlays differ block by block. We confirm zoning for the exact parcel before you go under contract.

📋

Market-by-Market Complexity

Phoenix: no citywide cap, $250 fee, relatively simple. South Lake Tahoe: ~900-permit cap, annual renewal, strict guest age requirements. Oahu: outside resort zones, effectively unpermittable — up to $10,000/day fine. We know which markets are actually investable.

📄

Full Document Filing

Proof of ownership, entity docs, liability insurance binder ($500K–$1M), state tax registration, floor plan, local contact requirement, neighbor notice via certified mail where required. We compile and file all of it.

📅

Renewal Calendar

Several jurisdictions only accept renewals during a narrow annual window — miss it and you may lose the permit entirely. The moment your permit is issued, we calendar every renewal deadline for you.

DIY: highly variable — weeks of research → With E2E: document uploads + approvals only
One wrong assumption about zoning or permit caps can sink an entire investment thesis. This step isn't optional — it's the difference between a legal STR and a liability. We've navigated it in four markets.
